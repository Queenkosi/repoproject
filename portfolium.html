Publication

When Tables Turn.

When Tables Turn is my first published book, a beautiful collection of soul-searching, poignant and prophetic poems with clear message. When Tables Turn is both piquant and realistic, an incisive commentary on the vicissitudes of life. Reading When Tables Turn will make you humble, sober but hopeful. It is a must read!

Praises for When Tables Turn

Dr. John Otu (HOD, Dept of English & Literary Studies, AE-FUNAI)

Most part of the volme is good, with some lilting lines that could culminate into great poetry in the near future....
The last poem in the volume is my idea of a good poem structurally-you could see how she artistically summed up the significant poetic symbols that run through the entire canvas of the poetry without any dull moment or weakness in the artistry.
Kosisochukwu is a new poetic voice that is distinct...alluring.

Dr. Onyebuchi Nwosu (Lecturer, Dept. of English & Literary Studies, AE-FUNAI)

The verses in many ways convey the inevitable paradox of life where conditions are constantly oscillating....It then behooves humanity not to be startled as every situation definitely has its tipping point.
Kosisochukwu's voice, though an emerging one, is evocative, realistic and quite promising.

David Nwafor (Creative writer, translator and proofreader)

When Tables Turn is a raw and engaging collection of poetry embracing profound themes about life, hope, freedom and nature. The poet painted the realities of life with words and this leaves the reader enthralled by her diction, figurative words and poetic devices employed.
When Tables Turn is a fascinating poetry collection that one should not afford to miss. I strongly recommend it.
